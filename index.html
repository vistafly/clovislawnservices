Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our lawn care services.

Our lawn mowing service in Clovis starts at $65 per visit for weekly mowing, $80 for biweekly, and $110 for monthly service. These prices include mowing, edging, and blowing. Every lawn is different, so we're happy to provide a custom quote for your property.

In the Fresno and Clovis area, we recommend weekly mowing during the growing season (spring through fall) to keep your lawn healthy and looking its best. During winter months, biweekly or monthly service is usually sufficient. Our weekly plan at $65/visit is the most popular choice among our customers.

Aeration creates small holes in your lawn to allow air, water, and nutrients to reach the grass roots. Overseeding fills in thin or bare spots with fresh grass seed. Together, they promote thicker, healthier lawn growth — especially important in the Central Valley's hot, dry climate where soil compaction is common.
